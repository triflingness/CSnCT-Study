2. Dynamic Programmingì˜ ì›ë˜ ë°©ë²•ì¸ Bottom-up ë°©ì‹ìœ¼ë¡œ í•´ê²°í•œë‹¤.

def binomial (n, r):

	for i in range(n+1):
		for j in range(r+1):
